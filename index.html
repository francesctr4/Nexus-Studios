Summary

In this RPG we will play a university student who, due to the temptation of using a new super powerful AI, ends up being absorbed by the computer. The student will appear in an unknown world where he will have to find his way out.

Prehistory

You go back to the time when humans began to show their superiority over other living beings on the planet.

Medieval

You travel to the medieval world, where knights and wizards faced very dangerous enemies.

Apocalypse

The end of the world, the human race has collapsed.

Cyberpunk Future

You arrive in the future, where the human race has reached a high level of technology and has managed to apply it to their bodies.

Group information

Nexus Studios is a group created in 2023 by several students of the CITM of Terrassa, with the aim of creating an RPG videogame for the subject Project II.

The video game in question was Dragged Off Time, a game that was created from the ideas of the whole group.
